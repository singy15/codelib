#### Vector calclulation

```
(defmethod crpd ((a vector2) (b vector2))
  "Calc cross-product of two vector2"
  (- (* (x a) (y b)) (* (y a) (x b))))

(defmethod dtpd ((a vector2) (b vector2))
  "Calc dot-product of two vector2"
  (+ (* (x a) (x b)) (* (y a) (y b))))
```
